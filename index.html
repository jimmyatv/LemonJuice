Our Goals

As a green-designed eco-lodge, we are fervently committed to sustainable tourism and the protection of natural resources. Since Maria Bay resort inception seven years ago, we have provided economic stimulation; employed hundreds of local tradesmen and craftsmen; promoted tourism; and laid miles of pipelines providing potable water to our neighboring communities. With thoughtful donations from many of our generous guests, we have been able to provide much needed educational supplies to 100 children in our immediate area. In the future, we hope to continue and further expand our commitment to the welfare of our community.
